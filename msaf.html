Overview

MSAF generates 3D pseudo-labels for autonomous driving without any human annotation. Given surround-view camera images and a LiDAR point cloud, an open-vocabulary 2D detector produces per-object segmentation masks. LiDAR points inside each mask are extracted, aggregated across cameras, and fitted into 3D bounding boxes through closed-form geometric stages. The pseudo-labels train a standard 3D detector (CenterPoint), achieving 31.4% mAP on nuScenes — surpassing OV-SCAN while running 72× faster.
